RRB Technician Recruitment 2026: Complete Guide & Online Application

Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has officially announced the massive recruitment drive for the post of Technician (Grade I and Grade III) for the year 2026. This is a golden opportunity for millions of aspirants holding ITI, Diploma, or Degree qualifications who wish to secure a prestigious Central Government job in the Indian Railways.

Important Dates for RRB Technician 2026

Before proceeding with your application, it is absolutely crucial that you note down the following important dates. Missing a deadline can result in instant disqualification.

Event Date
Official Notification Release 09 March 2026
Start Date for Online Registration 09 March 2026
Last Date to Apply Online 08 April 2026
Last Date for Fee Payment 08 April 2026
CBT-1 Exam Date October / November 2026

Application Fee Breakdown

The Railway Recruitment Board requires an application fee to process your candidacy. However, RRB often provides partial refunds to candidates who actually appear for the Computer Based Test (CBT).

  • General / OBC / EWS: ₹500/- (₹400 will be refunded after appearing in CBT-1)
  • SC / ST / PH / Female Candidates: ₹250/- (Full ₹250 will be refunded after appearing in CBT-1)
  • Payment Mode: You can pay the application fee securely using a Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking, or UPI infrastructure.

Eligibility Criteria & Age Limit

Aspirants must strictly adhere to the RRB guidelines regarding age and educational qualifications. The age will be calculated as of 01 July 2026.

  • Minimum Age: 18 Years
  • Maximum Age (Technician Grade I Signal): 36 Years
  • Maximum Age (Technician Grade III): 33 Years
  • Age relaxation is applicable as per the official RRB Technician Recruitment Rules (e.g., 3 years for OBC, 5 years for SC/ST).

Educational Qualifications

  • For Technician Grade I (Signal): Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) in Physics, Electronics, Computer Science, IT, or Instrumentation from a recognized university. OR a 3-year Diploma in Engineering in the respective fields.
  • For Technician Grade III (Various Trades): Class 10th (Matriculation) passed with an ITI Certificate in the related trade (NCVT/SCVT). OR 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics (specifically for Signal and Telecom roles).

Selection Process

The journey to becoming an RRB Technician involves multiple rigorous stages designed to test your knowledge, technical skills, and physical endurance:

  1. Computer Based Test (CBT): A comprehensive online examination consisting of multiple-choice questions (MCQs) covering Mathematics, General Intelligence & Reasoning, Basic Science & Engineering, and General Awareness.
  2. Document Verification (DV): Candidates who clear the CBT will be called to present their original mark sheets, ITI certificates, caste certificates, and identity proofs.
  3. Medical Examination: Indian Railways has strict medical standards. Candidates must pass the specific medical vision test (e.g., A3, B1, B2) as required for their designated post.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Apply Online

Avoid common mistakes that lead to application rejection! Follow these detailed steps to submit a flawless application:

  • Step 1: Visit the official regional RRB portal (e.g., RRB Allahabad, RRB Mumbai). Do not apply through third-party unverified sites.
  • Step 2: Click on the "CEN 02/2026 Technician Apply Online" link.
  • Step 3: Complete your basic registration. You will need an active mobile number and an active email address to receive your OTP and Registration Number.
  • Step 4: Log in with your new Registration Number and Date of Birth to fill out the detailed form.
  • Step 5: Upload your recent passport-sized photograph (with a white/light background) and signature. Pay close attention to the file size (usually 20KB-50KB). Applications with blurred photos are strictly rejected.
  • Step 6: Select your RRB Zone carefully. Once chosen, you cannot modify your regional zone.
  • Step 7: Proceed to the payment gateway and pay the requisite fee.
  • Step 8: Submit the application. Immediately download and print a physical copy of the submitted form. You will need this printout during Document Verification.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Will there be a CBT-2 for RRB Technician 2026?

As per the latest revised patterns, RRB has merged the examination for certain posts into a single CBT, followed immediately by Document Verification. Always verify the syllabus annexure for your specific trade.

I am in the final year of my ITI. Can I apply?

No. Candidates must possess the final passing certificate/mark sheet of their educational qualification as of the closing date of the online application.

Is the application fee refundable?

Yes. A partial or full refund is provided to candidates who actually sit for the CBT examination. The refund is credited back to the original payment source or the bank account provided during registration.
